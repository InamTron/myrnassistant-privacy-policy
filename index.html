Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy – My RN Assistant Last updated: January 2026 My RN Assistant (“the App”) is a free clinical assessment application designed for nursing students and individual registered nurses. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information. Information We Collect 1. Information You Provide ● Patient assessment data, notes, and clinical inputs entered by the user ● Media captured by the user (photos or audio) when using app features This data is stored locally on the device and is intended solely for the user’s educational or professional use. 2. Camera and Media Access The App requests access to: ● Camera – to document assessments or wounds ● Photo library / storage – to save or retrieve assessment-related media The App does not access the camera or media library without explicit user action. 3. Analytics and Usage Data To improve app stability, performance, and usability, the App collects limited, non-identifying analytics data, including: ● App usage events ● Operating system and app version ● Device type ● Approximate city-level location (derived from IP, not GPS) Analytics data is collected using a third-party analytics service and is used only to understand how the App is used and to improve functionality. 4. Purchases The App offers optional in-app purchases to unlock additional features. ● Purchases are processed by the platform provider (Google Play Store or Apple App Store) ● The App does not receive or store payment card details ● Purchase validation is handled through a third-party service Information We Do Not Collect ● No precise location (GPS) ● No contacts ● No background audio or video ● No biometric identifiers ● No advertising identifiers ● No personal identifiers beyond what the user voluntarily enters Data Use Collected information is used to: ● Provide and maintain app functionality ● Improve stability and user experience ● Enable optional feature unlocks ● Diagnose errors and crashes Data is never sold or used for advertising purposes. Data Storage and Security The App uses reasonable technical measures to protect stored data. Sensitive clinical information is intended to remain on the user’s device unless the user explicitly exports or shares it. Children’s Privacy The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Your Choices ● You may deny camera or media permissions; some features may not function ● You may uninstall the App at any time to stop all data collection ● You may manage in-app purchases through your platform’s account settings Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above. Contact If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the app developer through the app’s official distribution page.
